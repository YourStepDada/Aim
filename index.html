What's New

  • New School Canteen

    The food options and responsibilities to consider, canteens must be better organised and more efficient than they have been in the past. Our new canteen now has colourful banners hanging from the ceiling, each representing a different school value. Our simplistic menu now displayed on large screens on the walls provide even more information for what allergens that food has, what are the ingredients and their calories. We will also take card payments which will eliminate the fuss of money handling, making it less complicated to students. At AIM, we are leaders for tomorrow.
